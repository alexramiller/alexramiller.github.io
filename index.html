About

I am a PhD student in City and Regional Planning at the University of California Berkeley, and Graduate Student Researcher at the Terner Center for Housing Innovation and Urban Displacement Project. My research focuses on the relationship between property ownership and residential mobility, with a particular focus on the causes of eviction and other forms of residential displacement. I hold a BA in Geography and Economics from Macalester College and an MA in Geography from the University of Washington.

I have also consulted on several policy research and data analysis projects. These include an ongoing analysis of pandemic-related rent debt for PolicyLink and a displacement monitoring dashboard for the City of Seattle.
