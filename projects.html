Seattle Displacement Risk Indicators

2020
Seattle, Washington
Seattle Office of Planning and Community Development

This dashboard brings together demographic and market data - from sources including US Census microdata, King County tax assessment records, and City of Seattle permit records - to construct dynamic indicators of displacement risk across Seattle neighborhoods. This dashboard was constructed for the City of Seattle Office of Planning and Community Development's Equitable Development Monitoring Program.

The Evictions Study Map

2020
Seattle, Washington
The Evictions Study

This interactive map dashboard demonstrates the spatial distribution of evictions and neighborhood demographic characteristics in Washington State, with a focus on the Seattle-Tacoma-Bellevue and Bellingham metropolitan areas. This tools allows users to compare counts, rates, and relative risks of eviction with demographic characteristics at various geographic scales, including counties, census tracts, and state legistlative districts. The dashboard was built with R Shiny.
See the map.

The State of Evictions: Results from the University of Washington Evictions Project

2019
Seattle, Washington
The Evictions Study

This report presents the preliminary findings of The Evictions Study, using data collected from scraped court records in Washington State. The report demonstrates the volume of eviction filings between different counties, and also points to the existence of vast disparities in eviction risk by race and gender for households in the Seattle metropolitan area.
